Electricity Consumption Carbon Footprint

Residential electricity use in 2019 emitted over 9% of the United States total carbon emissions. Household appliances such as refrigerators, washing/drying machines, and space heating/cooling emit large amounts of CO2 into the atmosphere each day. Switching to cold water washes for clothes and implementing heat pumps or water coolers can help reduce your carbon footprint.
